Sereldic


Gallery

This is going to be a semi-rogue-like-dungeon-crawler (SRLDC - From here comes the game's title). At the moment it has:

  • Mage class
  • Movement
  • Ability to cast spells
  • Random spell system
  • An attack animation
  • Random Generation
  • A scrolling text box
  • A room editor
  • Gold
  • And a lot more coming!
